UNAIZAH

Unaizah is a city in the Al-Qassim Region of Saudi Arabia. Known for its rich history and cultural heritage, Unaizah is one of the oldest cities in the region. It is famous for its traditional markets, agricultural produce, particularly dates, and its annual festivals. The city offers a blend of historical sites and modern amenities, making it a unique place in the heart of Saudi Arabia.

BURAYDAH

Buraydah is the capital of the Al-Qassim Region in Saudi Arabia. It is the largest city in the region and serves as an important agricultural hub. Buraydah is known for its date farms, and the city hosts one of the largest date festivals in the world. It is also a center for commerce and trade, with modern infrastructure and facilities. Buraydah combines a rich cultural heritage with contemporary urban development.

ARASS

Ar Rass is a city in the Al-Qassim Region of Saudi Arabia. It is the third-largest city in the region and is known for its historical significance and cultural heritage. Ar Rass has a rich history dating back centuries and is home to various historical sites and landmarks. The city is also known for its agricultural activities, particularly in the production of dates and other crops. Ar Rass offers a blend of traditional and modern lifestyles, making it a noteworthy city in the Al-Qassim Region.
